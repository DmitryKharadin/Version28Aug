This website is maintained by a reincarnation of Jack Parsons, the magician and inventor.

It is intended for those seeking personal development through energy practice, or Chi practice, as well as emotional, mental, and spiritual development; primarily for those who, for some reasons, are staying with the society but have some time to dedicate to themselves.
The teachings and information are brough by online courses in Esoteric Yoga, Chi-gong and Western Esoterism, subscription materials, as well as public videos and articles.
It is indended to help the seekers in their journey, to be a meeting place for the like-minded people, a journal that refreshes one's practice and life; to be source to those who need it, in this format. The materials are geared toward practitioners who intend to develop their energy work and consciousness- awareness to be able to further proceed self-directed.
Personal consultations and coaching are available for investigating possible karmic blockages and overcoming of same.
